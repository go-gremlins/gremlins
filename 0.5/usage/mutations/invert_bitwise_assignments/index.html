    Invert bitwise assignments¶

    Invert bitwise assignments will perform inversions on basic bit operations, and it assigns the result of the two left and right operands to the left operand.

    Mutation table¶

    Original Mutated
    &= |=
    |= &=
    ^= &=
    &^= &=
    >>= <<=
    <<= >>=

    Examples¶

    a := 1
a &= 1

    a := 1
a |= 1
